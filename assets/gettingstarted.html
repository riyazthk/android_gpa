'CGPA & GPA Calculator for KEC' offers 4 different functionalities
1. GPA Calculator(with Arrear GPA Prediction)
2. CGPA Calculator
3. Internal Marks Calculator
4. External Marks Predictor
Note: Choose Your Department and Regulation properly to calculate CGPA,
for other three its optional

To Calculate GPA(with Arrear GPA Prediction):
1. Choose the appropriate credit points and Grades obtained for each subject
2. If any subject or Lab is NOT APPLICABLE then choose NA in Credit Points column
3. If you have Arrear/ Absent for any exam, choose RA/Ab appropriately
4. If you have choosen Arrear then predictions apprear for each grade

To Calculate CGPA:
1. Chooose the Department and Regulation Properly.
2.Enter the GPA obtained in each semester
3.You may leave any sem in the middle unfilled
4. Click on the button Calculate CGPA

To Calculate Internal Marks.
1.Input the marks obtained in each cycle test(max 50).
2.Input the Assignment Marks(max 10).
3.Input the Quiz/GD/Innovation mark(max 5)
4. Input the Attendance mark(max 5)

To Predict External Marks
1.Input one or more Internal/Continuous Assesment Marks obtained(max 50)
2. Click on 'Predict External Marks' button 3. Find the Prediction that is displayed for every mark given as Input
